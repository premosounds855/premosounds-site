PREMO
SOUNDS

Rapper. DJ. Producer. Portland born. Cambodia rooted. Vietnam based.

Premo Sounds is a genre-defying artist blending bass remixes, future beat, and baile-inspired rhythms into a sound shaped by city energy and global influence.

His music moves between rapping and spoken word — a confession booth exploring heartbreak, healing, identity, and growth.

His 2025 album THERAPY delivered a cinematic 10-track monologue of vulnerability. Followed by “Darkside” featuring SonaOne and the dancefloor-ready “Candy Clouds” with JASI.

Performing across Southeast Asia and the United States, Premo continues building a cross-cultural sound that refuses to stay in one lane.
