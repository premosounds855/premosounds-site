About Me

Premo Sounds is a rapper, DJ, and producer originally from Portland, Oregon, of Cambodian descent, now based in Vietnam. Bridging continents and cultures, his sound reflects a global perspective shaped by both the Pacific Northwest and Southeast Asia. Blending bass remixes, future beat, and baile-inspired rhythms, Premo creates music that moves between the underground and the dancefloor. His style carries an unmistakable urban energy — raw, cinematic, and emotionally charged. More than just club records, his music often feels like a confession booth. Shifting between rapping and spoken word, Premo explores themes of heartbreak, healing, identity, and growth. Each release captures a moment — unfiltered and intentional. His 2025 album THERAPY stands as a 10-track cinematic monologue of vulnerability and self-reflection. In 2026, he followed with “Darkside,” featuring SonaOne, expanding his collaborative reach. Earlier works like “Candy Clouds” with JASI showcased his ability to craft dancefloor-ready hip-house records, while tracks such as “MAF (Mad As F*ck),” “BLOODLINE,” and “Like Me” further define his genre-defying catalog. Premo Sounds has performed across Southeast Asia and the United States, maintaining a strong presence in Ho Chi Minh City and Phnom Penh. His collaborations with artists like Joka3iz and PRSTN demonstrate his commitment to building cross-cultural creative bridges. Available on Spotify, SoundCloud, and Apple Music, Premo Sounds continues to evolve — merging rhythm, emotion, and identity into a sound that refuses to sit in one lane.
